BBA PROGRAM

The list of candidates selected to get admission to BBA Program is given under the terms & conditions for admissions mentioned below:

 

  • Selected candidates are requested to remember the Student ID for payment of Admission Fee and to complete the admission procedure. Once you take admission at UIU, this ID will be your Student ID throughout your academic life at UIU.
  • Selected candidates are advised to take admission by paying the requisite Admission Fee (Tk. 15,000) and Caution Money for ID Card (Tk. 2,000 – refundable) by June 10, 2020 (Wednesday) June 21, 2020 (Sunday). For payment procedure, please visit: http://www.uiu.ac.bd/payment-instruction-admission-fee-summer-2020/. For any query, please call: 01914001470, 01711665055, 01745379745.
  • Soon after reopening of the University, candidates would be required to submit the attested photocopies of all Certificates, Testimonials & Transcripts/Mark Sheets, National ID Card/Birth Registration Certificate/Copy of Passport of the candidate, National ID Card of Father/Mother, 2 (two) copies of passport-sized photograph and 1 (one) copy of stamp-sized photograph (which is uploaded online) to the Admissions Office. Please note that discrepancy in any document of a student may lead to cancellation of admission of the student.
  • The Summer 2020 Semester will start in the 1st week of July 2020 and the classes will be conducted online until the Govt. opens the educational institution. The information about registration of courses, procedure to participate in the online classes and starting date of classes for Summer 2020 Semester will be notified later on the UIU Website (uiu.ac.bd).
